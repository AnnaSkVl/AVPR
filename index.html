interior & exterior

The managing blok is clear and functional.

In addition, the control centre is positioned in such a way that it is easily accessible to both the driver and passenger, as well as to the rear occupants.
In this way, AVTR establishes a close biometric link with the driver and helps to increase situational awareness.
The rear of the seats and the interior of the roof, are made from a special textile that changes colour. Depending on the lighting, the textile can be a dark blue or a subtle light blue.
But in addition, the eco-friendliness of the electric car's interior is enhanced by the use of vegan leather.
